# TON Compass Demo Script

## 20-second version

TON Compass is a guided first-move app for TON DeFi. Instead of dropping new users into a raw swap terminal, it recommends the right route, shows a live STON.fi quote before wallet connection, executes through the official STON.fi widget, and then offers a Tonstakers-ready continuation with Telegram follow-up. The result feels like the start of a TON journey, not a one-screen embed.

## 45-second version

TON Compass solves a simple but real problem: first-time TON users do not need a full DeFi terminal on their first interaction. They need a clear route.

The app starts with a Journey Builder that maps the user profile to the right entry mode. Then it adds a readiness layer so the user understands fees, slippage, and what they are about to do before they connect a wallet. Under that, the Decision Lab pulls live official STON.fi quote data and can turn a wallet address into the next recommended route. The actual swap still happens through the official STON.fi widget, so the execution layer is real. After the trade, the app offers a Tonstakers continuation planner and a Telegram-ready Compass Pro layer, so the experience does not end at the transaction.

## 60-second demo walkthrough

1. Open the live app:
   `https://forlearningandcerts.sellsystems.agency:49184/ton-compass/index.html`
2. Start with the framing:
   “Most first-time TON users do not need a raw DeFi terminal. They need one clear first move.”
3. Show the Journey Builder:
   pick `Learn safely`, `First on-chain action`, and `Small test amount`
4. Click `Apply recommendation` and show that the app lands on `Learn before you swap`
5. Show the readiness block and explain:
   “Before wallet connection, the app slows the user down and makes the first swap legible.”
6. Point to the live STON.fi widget and explain:
   “Execution is real here. TON Compass does not fake the swap.”
7. Scroll into the `Decision Lab` and show the live `TON → STON` quote
8. Switch the quote to `TON → tsTON` and explain:
   “The product does not stop at UI copy. It checks the route against live protocol data.”
9. Paste a wallet address and show the wallet-aware recommendation:
   “Now the app can turn balances into the next route instead of giving every user the same generic flow.”
10. Point to the Tonstakers planner and explain:
   “The continuation is honest too. It shows live TON ↔ tsTON market context and official Tonstakers constraints.”
11. Copy the route link or point to `Send route to Telegram` and explain:
   “This is where the retention and subscription layer starts.”
12. Close with:
   “TON Compass is not a swap clone. It is an onboarding and decision product on top of STON.fi.”

## Recording order

1. Start at the top of the page with the planner and widget both visible.
2. Do not scroll immediately; show the first-screen discipline first.
3. Scroll once to the Decision Lab and keep the cursor calm.
4. Show one quote switch, one wallet paste, and one Tonstakers reference.
5. End on the Telegram continuity cue, not in the middle of the page.

## On-screen talking points

- “Route first, not terminal first.”
- “Live quote before wallet prompt.”
- “Wallet-aware recommendation from official STON.fi data.”
- “Tonstakers continuation with real market context.”
- “Telegram continuity for retention and future subscription.”
