# TON Compass Demo Script

## 20-second version

TON Compass is a guided first-move app for TON DeFi. Instead of dropping new users into a raw swap terminal, it recommends the right route, gives them a readiness check before wallet connection, executes through the official STON.fi widget, and then offers a clear next step through Tonstakers. The result is a small app that already feels like the start of a real TON journey.

## 45-second version

TON Compass solves a simple but real problem: first-time TON users do not need a full DeFi terminal on their first interaction. They need a clear route.

The app starts with a Journey Builder that maps the user profile to the right entry mode. Then it adds a readiness layer so the user understands fees, slippage, and what they are about to do before they connect a wallet. The actual swap still happens through the official STON.fi widget, so the execution layer is real. After the trade, the app offers a Tonstakers-ready continuation path, so the experience does not end at the transaction.

## 60-second demo walkthrough

1. Open the live app:
   `https://forlearningandcerts.sellsystems.agency:49184/ton-compass/index.html`
2. Start with the framing:
   “Most first-time TON users do not need a raw DeFi terminal. They need one clear first move.”
3. Show the Journey Builder:
   pick `Learn safely`, `First on-chain action`, and `Small test amount`
4. Click `Apply recommendation` and show that the app lands on `Learn before you swap`
5. Show the readiness block and explain:
   “Before wallet connection, the app slows the user down and makes the first swap legible.”
6. Scroll slightly into the live STON.fi panel and point out that execution is real, not mocked
7. Copy the route link to show shareable onboarding presets
8. Point to the Tonstakers continuation card and explain:
   “After the first swap, the user gets a credible TON-native next step instead of a dead end.”
9. Close with:
   “TON Compass is not a swap clone. It is an onboarding product on top of STON.fi.”

TON Compass proves that the fastest useful TON product is not another generic DeFi surface. It is the missing first layer that turns a live STON.fi swap into a user journey.
