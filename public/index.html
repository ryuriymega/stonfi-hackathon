Hold TON? Roll into staking when the user is ready.

Tonstakers is TON liquid staking. Their official FAQ says staking starts from 1 TON, but users should keep at least 2.2 TON in wallet because the contract needs roughly 1.2 TON reserved for fees.

  • Use the official app only: app.tonstakers.com
  • Keep tsTON in the wallet for future withdrawals
  • Present staking as an optional second move, not a forced path
